FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why the storks and how it could be used?

In Ukrainian mythology, the white stork symbolises happiness and love to the homeland, it nests only near those houses where kindly and friendly people live. Our NFT collection is a digital asset that represents culture of our nation.


When will the collection be released?

Our collection of 3,000 NFT Art-objects will be released on market and be available for all caring buyers very soon. Follow us in social networks tove aware of updates!


Which percentage is sent to the foundation?

We care to send the maximum amount with the least commision to the charity. That’s why our collection is built on the Solana blockchain instead of the usual Ethereum. All of the incomes will be transferred directly to the Foundation.


Where will the money go?

All of the incomes from our NFT Art-Project will be transferred directly to the wallets of the fund "With God in heart". Money path is detailed here : https://github.com/StorkNFT?tab=projects


Crucial question - "Why should i invest in this project?"

Though, Stork of Freedom is charitable and all of the incomes go to help ordinary people affected by the war in Ukraine and all this brings : the growth of the community, their trust and their loyalty.

According to our road map, we will create another NFT project, which will have a large base of nft-enthusiasts, as a result of the previous project. Which will give an extremely large boost during the pre-mint stage, which is a significant advantage for the project development.

For people who want to join our community, it will be a desirable to buy our previous works to become a loyal fan of our concepts, accordingly, our past collection will increase in value, because all interrelated and people's rare nft will pump swiftly.


How can I make sure, that my money will go to the charity foundation?

Stork of Freedom is an Art-Project working in Partnership with the Charity Foundation “With God in heart”. All payments are going directly to their bank account and they will be declared.
